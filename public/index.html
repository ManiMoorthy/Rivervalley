About Us

We are a Level IV Rest Home licensed for 25 beds and also possess an additional classification of “Community Support Facility” (CSF) as classified by the Commonwealth of Massachusetts. Only Rest Homes with this CSF designation are able to admit mental health residents without gaining any other type of approval or waiver. The home is currently running 80% mental health residents through a mix of private pay and assistance.

About Northampton

Welcome to “Paradise City,” Northampton, Massachusetts. As residents of the Pioneer Valley, we enjoy the great natural beauty that surrounds us, from the winding Connecticut River that defines our eastern border, to the gentle mountains of the nearby Mount Holyoke and Mount Tom Ranges.
