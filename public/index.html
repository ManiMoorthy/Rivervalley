About Us

River Valley Rest Home is a Level IV Rest Home licensed for 25 beds with CSF (Community Support Facility) designation. With CSF designation, RVRH can admit residents with mental health without gaining any other approval or waiver. The home is currently running 60% mental health residents through a mix of private pay and assistance.

Resident Care
