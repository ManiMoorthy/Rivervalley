Church Services

Religious programs have always played an important role here at River Valley Rest Home.  We have several different church services available in different denominations.  We are happy to accommodate everyone who wants to participate, regardless of preference.

If you want to learn something new, we’re happy to teach you, and chances are, we’ll learn something from you, too. So if you have any special skills you’d like to share with us, we would love to incorporate them into our schedule.

Our therapy staff take pride in the performance of the entire facility.  We understand each patient has unique and special needs, so our in-house therapy program is completely customizable to meet them. This is a collaborative effort among our team members, who work with your physician to create a unique vision and treatment plan for you. We can even provide specialized equipment to aid in your treatment.
